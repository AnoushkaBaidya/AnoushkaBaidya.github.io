Wildfire

  • Project Overview
  • Explore a challenging simulation where a fire truck must navigate a complex maze to extinguish fires.
  • Added complexity with a unique twist: a hostile Wumpus is simultaneously setting new fires along the path.
  • Project Objectives
  • Developed advanced motion planning strategies to manage two entities: the fire-fighting truck and the fire-setting Wumpus.
  • Implemented planning solutions that handle dynamic and unpredictable changes within the environment.
  • Environment Challenges
  • The simulation includes a forest filled with multiple random obstacles.
  • Obstacles comprise dense areas filled with thick vegetation, trees, and weeds.
  • Unique shapes of obstacles modeled as giant tetrominoes, increasing navigation difficulty.
