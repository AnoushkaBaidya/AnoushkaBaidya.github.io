Autonomous Vehicle

  • Category: Motion Planning
  • Project date: Feb 2023 - Ongoing
  • CARLA | Python | Motion Planning
  • RRT* based local planner capable of generating smooth minimum acceleration trajectory. The trajectory complies to the non-holonomic constraints of the bicycle motion model.
  • Integrated RRT* with velocity obstacle to dodge dynnamic obstacles.
  • Tested against cross-road collision avoidance, head-on collision avoidance and overtaking maneuver.
